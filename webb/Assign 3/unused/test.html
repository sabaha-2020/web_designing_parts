Scientific Name : Panthera leo

Hi, I am lion, all are call me the 'King' of animals. I am roam the savannas and grasslands of the African continent, hunting cooperatively and raising cubs in prides.I have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. My coats are yellow-gold, and adult males have shaggy manes that range in color from blond to reddish-brown to black. The length and color of a my mane is likely determined by age, genetics and hormones. Young lions have light spotting on their coats that disappears as they grow.

Without our coats, mine and tiger bodies are so similar that only experts can tell us apart.

We are stand between 3.5 and 4 feet (1 and 1.2 meters) tall at the shoulder. Males grow to lengths of 10 feet (3 meters) and have a 2 to 3 foot (60 to 91 centimeter) tail. They weigh from 330 to 550 pounds (150 to 250 kilograms). Slightly smaller, females grow to lengths of 9 feet (2.7 meters) and weigh between 265 and 395 pounds.
